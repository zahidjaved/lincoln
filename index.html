What Do Lenders Look For?

Securing refinancing for commercial mortgages requires meeting specific eligibility criteria, varying among lenders and loan types. Here are key factors commonly considered for approval :

Financial Stability

Lenders deliberate on whether to grant you the green light for refinancing thorough a comprehensive investigation into your financial standing. Key factors that lenders assess include your credit score and global cash flow.

Property Performance

Beyond financial stability, lenders also cast their gaze upon the performance of the commercial property you intend to refinance. Critical metrics in their assessment include occupancy rates, rental income, property valuation, and lease terms.

Global Cash Flow

Global cash flow is a pivotal concept in refinancing as it offers a comprehensive view of a borrower's financial health. It encompasses not only the income generated by the specific property being refinanced but also all other income sources owned.

Business Plan

If the commercial property is part of a business operation, lenders may want to review the borrower's business plan to understand its future prospects and sustainability. Ensure that your offering memorandum is well crafted to reflect such.
